ABOUT

Ryan Beck is an author of science-fiction novels and short stories. He loves exciting and thought-provoking fiction of many different types. He lives in Iowa where he works as a bridge engineer. In his free time, Ryan is usually spending time with his wife and daughters, reading, or trying to learn a new skill.
